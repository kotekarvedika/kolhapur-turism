WELCOME TO KOLHAPUR

Kolhapur, known as the "Cultural Capital of Maharashtra" and "Dakshin Kashi," is a historic city 6
with a deep-rooted heritage spanning ancient temples, Maratha-era forts, and royal palaces.
Famous for the Mahalakshmi Temple, traditional wrestling (talim), and Kolhapuri chappals, it blends spiritual, martial, and artistic traditions.
